Who we are

A small firm with real presence on both sides

ZED Services is a France-based boutique advisory firm working at the intersection of institutional finance and African market realities. We work with entrepreneurs who have strong projects and need structured support to access financing, and with investors who want qualified exposure to West and Central Africa without going in blind.

01
Deal Structuring
We help structure transactions — equity, debt, blended finance — in a way that is credible to investors and adapted to local realities.
02
Capital Mobilization
We identify relevant financing sources — DFIs, diaspora investors, private family offices, grants — and support the fundraising process from preparation to close.
03
Terrain Intelligence
We have people on the ground in Abidjan, Bingerville, Yaoundé, Ebolowa and the Thiès region. This is where our edge comes from.
04
Operational Support
From administrative setup to partner coordination, we accompany entrepreneurs through execution as well as planning.
What we do

Our six capabilities

We are not a generalist firm. We focus on a few areas where we can genuinely add value and where our field presence makes a concrete difference.

01
Deal Structuring & Transaction Advisory
Investment memoranda, financial modelling and transaction structuring for DFIs, private investors and development finance actors. We work to institutional standard.
02
Capital Raising & Financing Strategy
Identification of the right capital sources — grants, debt, equity, blended finance, crowdfunding — and support through the fundraising process from preparation to close.
03
Africa Market Access & Intermediation
Deal sourcing and cross-border intermediation for investors looking for qualified opportunities in West and Central Africa. We do the groundwork.
04
Real Estate Advisory
Land acquisition support, project structuring and asset management for real estate investments in Côte d'Ivoire and Senegal.
05
Business Setup & Operations
Market entry support, local partner identification and operational accompaniment for entrepreneurs and investors establishing themselves in West Africa.
06
China–West Africa Trade
Sourcing, logistics and commercial structuring for import/export operations along the China–Côte d'Ivoire corridor.
How we work

Our approach

I
Listen
We take time to understand the project, the market context and what the entrepreneur actually needs. We ask questions before we propose anything.
II
Structure
We help build the financial and legal architecture of the transaction — adapted to the project's reality, not copied from a template.
III
Connect
We introduce the project to relevant capital and partners. We prefer targeted introductions to the right people over broad outreach.
IV
Follow through
We try to stay involved as things move forward. We know implementation is where most things get complicated, and we do not disappear at that stage.
