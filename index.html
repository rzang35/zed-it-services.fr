Who we are

A boutique deal structuring firm rooted in Africa

ZED Services is a France-based advisory firm specializing in deal structuring, project financing, and business development for entrepreneurs and investors across West and Central Africa. Active since 2021.

We operate at the intersection of institutional finance and on-the-ground African realities — translating project potential into bankable opportunities, and connecting entrepreneurs with the capital and partners they need to grow.

With deep networks in Côte d'Ivoire, Cameroon, Senegal and beyond, and institutional expertise across audit, private equity and impact finance, we bring a rare combination of rigor and proximity.

⚙
Deal Structuring
We structure transactions — equity, debt, blended finance — that meet both investor standards and local realities.
🔗
Capital Mobilization
From DFIs to diaspora investors, we identify and approach the right capital sources for each project profile.
🌍
Terrain Intelligence
On-the-ground networks in Abidjan, Bingerville, Thiès Region, Yaoundé and Ebolowa — real contacts, real market knowledge.

Operational Support
From administrative setup to business development, we accompany entrepreneurs through execution, not just planning.
